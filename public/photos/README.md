# 📸 Photos

Drop your photos here:

- `photo1.jpg` — Used in the **First Meet** section (Section 3) and memory modal for "First Meet" star
- `photo2.jpg` — Used in the **Then & Now** section (Section 6 — "Now" photo)

Both photos also need `photo1.jpg` for the "Then" column in Then & Now.

## Tips
- Recommended aspect ratio: **3:4** (portrait) for best display
- JPG, PNG, or WebP formats supported
- Update the paths in `src/config/memories.ts` if you rename them
